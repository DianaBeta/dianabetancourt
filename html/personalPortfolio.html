Personal Portfolio Case Study

Building experiences that communicate.

Project Brief
This Project consisted on the creation of a portfolio website for me, a UI/UX and Front-End web developer.
  • Challenge: UX/UI designers need an online presence and a showcase of the projects that they have done. In this particular case, my projects were in Github and Behance but I needed a website to showcase all my skills.
  • Target audience:Recruiters, UX or Digital product Design managers, Clients.
Research
Primary data
Was gathered through interviews with senior UX Designers and recruiters.
Secondary data
Was gathered through the study of other UX/UI portfolios.
